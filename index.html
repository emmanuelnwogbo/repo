For decades the Nigerian police especially the Special Anti Robbery Squad (SARS) have terrorized Nigerian youths. The #EndSARS movement is a movement by Nigeria youth for Nigerian youth to tackle police brutality and the systemic decay evident in the Nigerian police force. In light of this, thousands of youth across the nation have several peaceful protests to allay their grievances to the Nigerian Government. The endSARSmovement.com is a media repository dedicated to document and display the journey of Nigerian youths on their quest for real transformation and change.
